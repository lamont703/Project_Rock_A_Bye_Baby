Legislative Priorities

1

Due Process Protection

Mandate proper notice and opportunity to be heard in all family court proceedings, ending secret modifications and ex parte orders.

2

Equal Parenting Presumption

Establish legal presumption of equal parenting time for fit parents, removing financial incentives for denying custody.

3

Parental Alienation Recognition

Create legal remedies for parental alienation and hold parties accountable for interfering with parent-child relationships.

4

Judicial Accountability

Establish oversight mechanisms and consequences for judges who violate constitutional rights or ignore the law.

5

Financial Reform

Remove profit motives from family court decisions and eliminate financial incentives for prolonged litigation.
