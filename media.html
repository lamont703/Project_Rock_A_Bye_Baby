Story Angles & Key Themes

Constitutional Rights Violation

Due process denied through secret court proceedings and systematic violations of 14th Amendment rights.

  • Secret child support modifications without notice
  • Denied equal protection under the law
  • Constitutional challenges to family court system

Parental Alienation Crisis

Systematic campaign to alienate children from their father despite no evidence of unfitness.

  • Children used as financial leverage
  • Mental health impact on children
  • Third-party involvement in alienation

System Reform Needed

Family court system prioritizes revenue generation over children's welfare and constitutional rights.

  • Financial incentives driving court decisions
  • Lack of accountability for violations
  • Need for comprehensive reform

Human Interest Story

A father's 17-year journey fighting for his children's right to have both parents in their lives.

  • Personal sacrifice for children's wellbeing
  • Resilience in face of systematic injustice
  • Hope for future generations
