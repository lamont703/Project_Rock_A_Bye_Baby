The Hidden Cost of Family Court Injustice: When Children Become Casualties

January 2025 By Lamont Evans 8 min read

After 17 years of fighting within a broken system, the most devastating realization isn't the financial burden, the constitutional violations, or even the personal sacrifice. It's watching your children suffer from anxiety and depression because they don't understand why their father couldn't be more present in their lives. This is the human cost of a system that prioritizes revenue over relationships.
