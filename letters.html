Children's Welfare Personal

Featured Letter: January 27, 2013

TO: Talicia Townsend
FROM: Lamont Evans
DATE: January 27, 2013
RE: Settlement Discussion - Shared Parenting Agreement

Dear Talicia,

I am writing to you in hopes of reaching an amicable resolution regarding the custody and support of our children. The current arrangement has created financial hardship while preventing me from having meaningful parenting time with our children.

I am proposing a 50/50 shared custody arrangement that would eliminate the need for child support payments while ensuring both children have equal access to both parents. This arrangement would be in the best interests of our children and would allow us both to focus on co-parenting rather than court battles.

Please consider this proposal seriously. If I do not hear from you by February 25, 2013, I will have no choice but to proceed with litigation to secure my parental rights.

Sincerely,
Lamont Evans

This letter demonstrates good faith effort at resolution before litigation
